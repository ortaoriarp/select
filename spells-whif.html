Spellchart of Whiff Powers

Fundemental Whiff Powers

Fundemental Whiff powers has no orientation and can be used by both Sky and Fire Disciples.

Power Orientation Description
Push Neutral

The Disciple uses whiff to augment speed and strength, and charges across the battlefield towards a target. This culminates in a powerful collision that sends unprotected enemies flying backward, inflicting massive damage. The collision negates the mass of an enemy, allowing for even the largest of enemies to be sent flying backwards.

This power also compensates for impeding obstacles by allowing the Disciple to 'phase' through solid objects en route to their target. It can also be used on enemies who have been levitated by other whiff powers, and can multiply physics damage to send them off into the distance.

Pull Neutral

The Disciple can use Pull to generate a mass-lowering field, which levitates enemies into the air. While aloft, enemies will slowly drift towards the Disciple, allowing any allies to finish them off effortlessly. The Disciple can also combine the Pull effect with a Throw or Push to send enemies flying higher into the air or with Warp which will Disciple detonate the target. Pull inflicts a small amount of damage on foes with protection.

Shockwave Neutral

When used, the power sends out a series of explosive whiff impacts in front of the Disciple ignoring any obstacles. It can be unleashed along the ground to launch all enemies in its path into the air, or used against airborne targets. It is worth noting that when Shockwave hits a shielded target it will be stunned for a brief moment but won't be thrown into the air. This can be useful in stopping advancing enemies and allowing you to focus on this target with your allies.

Nova Neutral

Nova is a close-range shock wave power which utilizes the Disciple's high-risk, high-reward combat system. While expending the remainder of your Whiff Barrier, Nova inflicts a high amount of damage to surrounding enemies, pushing them back and sending them soaring into the air. There is no cooldown--it can be used whenever the Disciple has certain amount of barrier remaining.

Throw Neutral

Throw is mostly used to keep opponents at a distance by either launching them away or knocking them over, damaging them and rendering them helpless.

In certain areas Throw may knock enemies off ledges, making them fall to their death. If used near destructible objects, Throw can break them and hit enemies with debris. Any moveable objects can also be thrown into them, injuring them further. In enclosed quarters, Advanced and Master Throw are strong enough to punch enemies against walls and cause significant damage.

Warp Neutral

Warp works by creating rapidly shifting mass effect fields that shred a target apart. It starts the tearing process from any material--like armors or weapons, then it jumps to the target itself causes damage over time. Warp also can be used as enchanment so Disciple can enchant arrows that can tear through an armor or even Whiff Barrier.

Another advantage of Warp is negating some genetical phenomena like Orc's Hyper-rage or Blood-Elf's meta-cast.
Singularity Neutral

This gravitational power sucks multiple enemies within a radius to a single area, leaving them floating helplessly and vulnerable to attack. It can also attract objects from the environment, such as crates or pieces of furniture; enemies will take damage if they collide with other solid objects in the Singularity field.

In Advanced level, The Disciple can use Singularity field as black hole which bends time and space continuum randomly and Singularity field slow or accelerate time flow.

In Master level, Singularity field forms a worm hole that can be used to travel in time and space.

Whiff Barrier Neutral

The Disciple has a semi-passive gravitational field that is generating counter gravity on sub-atomic level. Since whiff itself acts in microcosmic fields, it can effect phase power along with the material itself. This thin mass effect field nullifies atomic orbital movement speed, so The Disciple won't be effected as long as barrier is enforced.

Sky Desciple Whiff Powers

Sky Discipline is widely used by Followers of Akanda, very first student of Narod--the First Whiff Master, mostly serves for freedom and equity.

Power Orientation Description
Ignore Truth Full Sky

Ignore Truth ability uses a whiff power on someone-else for declining any input considered as fact. If target experienced something real, the Disciple can force the target's brain for ignore or remove the input data.

Detect Fire Full Sky

The Disciple can detect surrounding Fire Oriented Whiff Power. If the ability is precasted, Whiff Barrier of the Disciple will provide extre defense against FOWP.

Grace Full Sky

The Disciple can offer healing anyone who in need of it. With Grace ability, caster can re-initiate the celluar regeneration of the target, like a lizard. Grace can be used on self, if only the Whiff-Barrier is breached.

Whip Full Sky

This ability allows the Disciple to creat an temproary whip-alike air-vortex. That vortex sucks out the air on the tip of it and siphons out the air at the end, that vacuum effect can be used for destablizing the enemy or disarming him.

Skybridge Full Sky

The Disciple can create an invisible bridge with continous air flow, that allows the Disciple and his party members to walk on it. As an addition, both the Disciple and his party members walk faster on the bridge.

Levitate Full Sky

The Disciple can levitate himself in time of need. As a result of the Disciple's high-risk/high-reward combat dynamic, while levitated Whiff-Shield stays on the ground and he becomes more vulnerable. As reward, he can keep fighting and the Whiff-Shield on the ground becomes a Whiff-Wall so other party members can use it as a cover.

Fire Disciple Whiff Powers

Fire Discipline is widely used by Followers of Einhelm, son and most powerful student of Narod--the First Whiff Master, mostly serves for order and discipline.

Power Orientation Description
Enforce Lie Full Fire

Enforce Lie ability uses a whiff power on someone-else for forcing lie about any input considered as fact. If target experienced something real, the Disciple can force the target's brain for lie about or manipulate the input data.

Detect Sky Full Fire

The Disciple can detect surrounding Sky Oriented Whiff Power. If the ability is precasted, Whiff Barrier of the Disciple will provide extre defense against SOWP.

Disease Full Fire

The Disciple can punish anyone who is against the order. With Disease ability, caster can stop the immune system of the target, like Human Immunodeficiency Viruses. Disease can be used on another Disciple, if only the his Whiff-Barrier is breached.

Choke Full Fire

This ability allows the Disciple to creat an temproary mini-fire in the trachea of the target. That fire burns out the oxygen and target won't be able to breath as long as fire exist. Could be fatal.

Microcombust Full Fire

The Disciple can create a microwave orb with sub-atomic resonance, that allows him melt down any kind of objects, for examples locks or door knobs. Microcombust also can be use for ignition.

Fireflow Full Fire

The Disciple transforms himself into wave of fire and travels from point-to-point on battle field. As a result of the Disciple's high-risk/high-reward combat dynamic, while transformed he can go through the any object that have air-gaps. If the Disciple wants to go through an enemy, he can flow into his direction and on contact enemy will suffer from fire damage but not explosion. As a risk, contating with an enemy while casting Fireflow causes damage to the Disciple's himself for the 33% of the damage done with this spell.
